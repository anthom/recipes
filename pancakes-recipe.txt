Ingredients:
 - 2 cups all-purpose flour
 - 2 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1/4 teaspoon salt
 - 1 tablespoon sugar, optional
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 1/2 to 2 cups milk
 - 2 tablespoons melted and cooled butter (optional), plus unmelted butter for cooking, or use neutral oil

Directions:

1. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium-low heat. 
2. In a bowl, mix together dry ingredients. 
3. In another bowl, whisk togeher eggs, milk and cooled melted butter.
4. Stir the liquid into dry ingredients. Add more milk if it seems thick.
3. Grease a skillet, then put 1/4 cup of batter on hot skillet. Flip pancakes after bubbles rise to surface and bottoms brown, after 2 to 4 minutes.
4. Cook until second side is lightly browned. Serve, or hold on an ovenproof plate in a 200-degree oven for up to 15 minutes.
